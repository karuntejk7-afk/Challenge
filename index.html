Pesticide-Free Cultivation Tips

Use Organic Fertilizers

Boost soil health naturally with compost, manure, and crop residues.

Companion Planting

Grow crops together that deter pests and improve biodiversity.

Biological Control

Encourage beneficial insects like ladybugs to manage pests.

Crop Rotation

Prevent soil-borne diseases by rotating crops each season.

Natural Sprays

Use neem oil or garlic spray to reduce pest infestations safely.
